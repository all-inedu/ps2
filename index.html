The Journey

1

Ever since we started, it's clear that we want to achieve two things: developing a useful product based on solar energy and spreading awareness on the underutilized renewable energy. For the former, we designed a power bank, fitted with lithium-Ion batteries, and charged by a miniature solar panel.

Early renders

2

To make our prototype affordable, all the components were sourced locally, except the case which was 3D printed. We experienced some issues during assembly. Most notably, is when the battery caught on fire, which was a major setback.
